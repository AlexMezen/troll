Officer Broks

One day, the incredible happened in the peaceful town: A candy store was robbed! A police officer named Broks was assigned to investigate the case. Despite his impressive appearance, Broks was known for his sense of humor and his love for pig's trotters, which he always carried in his holster. When he was upset, he would take out the pig's trotters and start chewing on them to calm himself down.


When Broks arrived at the scene of the robbery, he saw a group of elves trying to escape with all the candy from the store. He approached them and, while humorously picking his teeth with the pig's trotters, shouted, "Stop! Hands up – hats on the floor! What's going on here? Won't your pockets burst?!”

The elves were scared and giggling. They hadn’t expected such a funny policeman to show up. One of the elves approached Broks and explained that they just couldn’t resist the temptation of the candy.

Broks began to think. He himself had gotten into funny situations because of his love for pig's trotters. Instead of arresting the elves, he decided to help them.

"Let’s find a way to solve this differently," said Broks with a smile. "We can have a competition for the funniest dance, and the winner gets a box of candy from the store."

The elves loved the idea! They performed the funniest dances, and Broks joined them, adding his own funny moves. After the competition, the candy was returned to the store, and everyone went on their way. They had forgotten about the robbery but left with many funny memories and a box of candy for each elf.

From that day on, Broks and the elves became friends and occasionally entertained people in town with funny dance competitions, where one could always win candy and have a lot of fun.
